Welcome to SAGE: Your own medicine provider and reminder bot.

Sage offers you the right medicine according to your needs

  1. The user will provide his/her Name and Information.
  2. Then he/she will enter the disease he is suffering from.
  3. Sage will provide the needed medication and the dosage.
  4. User can also set an alarm which will notify when to take the medicines.
